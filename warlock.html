WARLOCK - SHADEBINDER

FRACTURE WITH FROST

Keen masters of destruction. Launching projectiles, freezing as many enemeies as possible, and shattering them with all in one fell swoop, the Shadebinder ensures no foe is left standing!

Below are some in depth discription of the abilities

Winter's Wrath

Shadebinders harness the power of Darkness to summon a Stasis staff

Shatter Shockwave

The Shadebinder detonates the crystals on their staff, creating a devasting Shockwave that shatters all nearby frozen enemies.

Penumbral Blast

Fires Stasis projectiles from their staff that explode on impact. Enemies hit by the crystalline shrapnel are immediately frozen solid.
